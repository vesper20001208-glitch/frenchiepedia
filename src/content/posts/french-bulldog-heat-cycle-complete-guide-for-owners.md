# French Bulldog Heat Cycle: Complete Guide for Owners

## Understanding the Frenchie Heat Cycle
Female French Bulldogs typically have their first heat cycle between 6-12 months of age. They cycle every 6 months thereafter.

## Signs Your Frenchie Is in Heat
- Swollen vulva (first visible sign)
- Bloody discharge (starts light, becomes heavier, then lightens)
- Increased urination (marking behavior)
- Behavior changes (clingy, restless, or irritable)
- Attracting male dogs from surprising distances

## The Four Stages
Proestrus (7-10 days): Vulva swells, bloody discharge. Not fertile.
Estrus (5-14 days): Fertile window. Keep separated from intact males.
Diestrus (60-90 days): Resting phase. False pregnancy possible.
Anestrus (4-5 months): No hormonal activity between cycles.

## Care During Heat
- Use doggie diapers for your home
- Keep her on leash at all times outside
- Avoid dog parks and public areas
- Keep her away from intact male dogs for 3-4 weeks

## When to Spay
Most vets recommend spaying after the first heat cycle (12-18 months) for proper development.

## When to Call the Vet
- Heat lasting more than 4 weeks
- Extremely heavy bleeding
- Signs of pain or distress
- No heat by 18 months of age
