# French Bulldog Sleeping Habits: What Is Normal?

## How Much Do French Bulldogs Sleep?
Puppies: 18-20 hours per day.
Adults: 12-14 hours per day.
Seniors: 14-16 hours per day.
Frenchies are champion sleepers - this is normal for the breed.

## Common Sleeping Positions
The Frenchie Flip (on back, legs up): feels completely safe.
The Donut (curled in a ball): conserves heat.
The Sploot (belly down, legs splayed): cooling position.
Under covers: mimics den behavior for security.

## Snoring
Most Frenchies snore - this is normal. See your vet if snoring suddenly gets louder or your Frenchie stops breathing during sleep.

## Creating a Good Sleep Environment
- Orthopedic bed for joint support
- Cool room temperature (65-70F / 18-21C)
- Consistent bedtime routine
- No food 2 hours before bed
- Potty break right before bed

## When to Worry
Consult your vet if: sleeping significantly more than usual, difficulty getting comfortable, restless sleep, sudden snoring increase.

A well-rested Frenchie is a happy, healthy Frenchie.
