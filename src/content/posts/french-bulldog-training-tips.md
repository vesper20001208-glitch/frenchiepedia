# French Bulldog Training Guide

French Bulldogs are intelligent but stubborn. Consistency is key.

## Potty Training
1. Establish a schedule
2. Designate a specific spot
3. Reward immediately when they go outside
4. Supervise indoors

## Basic Commands
- **Sit:** Hold treat above head, move backward
- **Stay:** Open palm, step back, reward
- **Come:** Crouch down, call enthusiastically

## Common Issues
- **Barking:** Address the cause
- **Chewing:** Provide appropriate toys
- **Separation Anxiety:** Short departures first
