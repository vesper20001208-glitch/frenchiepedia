### How long do French Bulldogs live?
The average lifespan is 10-12 years. With excellent care, some live to 14-15 years.

### Are French Bulldogs good with kids?
Yes, they are generally excellent with children. They are sturdy, patient, and affectionate. Always supervise interactions with young children.

### Do French Bulldogs shed?
Yes, they shed moderately year-round. They have a single coat and shed more than you might expect from a short-haired breed.

### Are French Bulldogs smart?
They are intelligent but stubborn. They ranked 109th out of 138 breeds in obedience, but they learn quickly when motivated with treats.

## Health Questions

### Do French Bulldogs have breathing problems?
Yes, due to brachycephalic airway syndrome (BOAS). Signs include snoring, loud breathing, and overheating easily. Severe cases may need surgery.

### What health problems do French Bulldogs have?
Common issues: BOAS, skin allergies, ear infections, cherry eye, hip dysplasia, IVDD, obesity, dental disease, and heart conditions.

### Why do French Bulldogs fart so much?
They swallow air when eating and drinking (aerophagia) combined with sensitive stomachs and food intolerances. Slow feeder bowls and limited ingredient diets help.

### Do all French Bulldogs need C-sections?
Over 90% of French Bulldogs require C-sections due to their large heads and narrow birth canals.

### How often should I take my Frenchie to the vet?
At least twice a year for checkups. More frequent visits if they have chronic health issues.

## Training Questions

### Are French Bulldogs easy to potty train?
They can be challenging due to their stubborn streak. Consistency, patience, and positive reinforcement are essential. Use the 30-minute potty schedule.

### Do French Bulldogs bark a lot?
They bark less than most small breeds. They are alert but not yappy. They will bark at doorbells and unusual sounds.

### Can French Bulldogs be left alone?
They can be left alone for 4-6 hours as adults, but they are prone to separation anxiety. Crate training helps.

## Care Questions

### What is the best food for a French Bulldog?
High-quality food with real meat as the first ingredient, preferably a limited ingredient diet for sensitive stomachs. Avoid fillers like corn and soy.

### How much exercise does a Frenchie need?
20-30 minutes of moderate exercise daily, split into two sessions. Avoid exercise in hot weather.

### How often should I clean my Frenchie's wrinkles?
Daily. Use unscented baby wipes or wrinkle wipes, then dry thoroughly to prevent infections.

### Do French Bulldogs need coats in winter?
Yes, they need coats in temperatures below 45°F (7°C). They have short coats and low body fat.
