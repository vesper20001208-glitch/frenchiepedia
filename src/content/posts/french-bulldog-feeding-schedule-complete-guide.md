# French Bulldog Feeding Schedule: Complete Guide

## French Bulldog Feeding by Age

### Puppy (8 weeks to 6 months)
3-4 meals per day. 1/4 to 1/2 cup per meal. High-quality puppy formula.

### Adolescent (6-12 months)
3 meals per day. 1/3 to 2/3 cup per meal. Transition to adult food at 10-12 months.

### Adult (1-7 years)
2 meals per day. 1/2 to 3/4 cup per meal. High-quality adult formula.

### Senior (7+ years)
2 meals per day. Adjust based on activity. Senior or joint-support diet.

## How Much to Feed by Weight
- 16-20 lbs: 1 to 1.25 cups per day (350-450 cal)
- 20-24 lbs: 1.25 to 1.5 cups per day (400-500 cal)
- 24-28 lbs: 1.5 to 1.75 cups per day (450-550 cal)

## Best Feeding Schedule
Option A: 7 AM breakfast, 5 PM dinner
Option B: 8 AM breakfast, 6 PM dinner
Keep meal times consistent for best digestion.

## Feeding Tips
- Use a slow feeder bowl to prevent gulping air
- Measure every meal with a measuring cup
- Wait 30 minutes after exercise before feeding
- Do not free-feed
- Change water at least twice daily

## Foods to Avoid
Chocolate, grapes, raisins, onions, garlic, xylitol, macadamia nuts, raw dough, alcohol, caffeine, avocado.

A consistent feeding schedule is the foundation of your Frenchie health.
