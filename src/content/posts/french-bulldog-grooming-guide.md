# French Bulldog Grooming Guide

Frenchies have short coats but specific grooming needs.

## Wrinkle Care (Most Important!)
1. Lift each wrinkle fold gently
2. Wipe with damp cloth
3. Dry thoroughly between each fold
4. Apply balm if needed

**Never leave moisture in wrinkles!**

## Coat Care
Brush 1-2 times per week. Frenchies shed moderately.

## Bathing
Every 4-6 weeks with hypoallergenic shampoo.

## Nail Trimming
Every 3-4 weeks. Trim small amounts.

## Ear Cleaning
Check weekly. Use vet-recommended cleaner.
