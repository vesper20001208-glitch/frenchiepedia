## Tips for Choosing

1. Say it out loud — will it be easy to call at the dog park?
2. Pick 1-2 syllables — Frenchies respond better to shorter names
3. Test it for a week — if it doesn't stick, try another
4. Avoid names that sound like commands (Kit, Sid, etc.)
