# French Bulldog Enrichment: 10 Fun Indoor Activities

## Why Enrichment Matters
A bored Frenchie is a destructive Frenchie. Mental stimulation tires them out faster than physical exercise.

## 1. Snuffle Mat Foraging
Hide kibble or treats in a snuffle mat. Your Frenchie uses their nose to find food. Great for slow eating too.

## 2. Frozen Kong Treats
Stuff a Kong with wet food or peanut butter, then freeze. Keeps your Frenchie occupied for 30-60 minutes.

## 3. Treasure Hunt
Hide small treats around the room and let your Frenchie find them. Start with visible treats.

## 4. Puzzle Toys
Food-dispensing puzzle toys challenge your Frenchie. Start with level 1 puzzles.

## 5. Trick Training
Teach new tricks: spin, shake, high-five, play dead. Five-minute sessions build skills.

## 6. Box Fort Destruction
Let your Frenchie shred an empty cardboard box with treats inside. Supervise to prevent swallowing large pieces.

## 7. Scent Work
Place a treat under one of three cups. Shuffle them and let your Frenchie find the treat.

## 8. Tug-of-War
A structured game of tug satisfies your Frenchie natural drive.

## 9. Flirt Pole Play
A flirt pole lets your Frenchie chase and pounce. Great for small spaces.

## 10. Name That Toy
Teach your Frenchie the names of their toys. Start with one toy, say its name, reward when they touch it.

## Daily Schedule
Morning: 10-minute snuffle mat. Midday: 5-minute training. Afternoon: Frozen Kong. Evening: 10-minute play.

A mentally stimulated Frenchie is a happy companion.
