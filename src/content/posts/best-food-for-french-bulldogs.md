# Best Dog Food for French Bulldogs

French Bulldogs have sensitive digestive systems and food allergies.

## What to Look For
- **High-Quality Protein** as first ingredient
- **Limited Ingredients** for sensitive stomachs
- **Omega Fatty Acids** for skin health

## Top Recommendations
1. Hill's Science Diet Sensitive Stomach - Best for digestion
2. Royal Canin French Bulldog - Breed-specific formula
3. Blue Buffalo Basics - Great for allergies
4. Taste of the Wild - High protein option

## Feeding Schedule
- Puppy: 3-4 meals/day
- Adult: 2 meals/day
- Senior: 2 meals/day + joint supplements

## Foods to Avoid
Grapes, raisins, onions, garlic, chocolate, xylitol
