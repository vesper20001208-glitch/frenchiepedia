# Complete French Bulldog Health Guide

French Bulldogs are one of the most beloved dog breeds, but they come with specific health needs.

## Common Health Issues

### Brachycephalic Airway Syndrome (BOAS)
French Bulldogs have flat faces and shortened airways.

**Signs:** Loud breathing, snoring, excessive panting
**Prevention:** Keep cool, avoid exercise in heat, maintain healthy weight

### Skin Allergies
Frenchies have sensitive skin prone to allergies.

**Symptoms:** Scratching, red skin, hair loss, ear infections

### IVDD (Back Problems)
Risk of disc disease in short-legged breeds.

**Prevention:** Use harness, limit jumping, healthy weight, ramps

## Regular Checkups
Take your Frenchie to the vet at least twice a year.

## When to See a Vet
Difficulty breathing, collapse, severe vomiting, signs of pain.
